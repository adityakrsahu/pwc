About PW Centres

Education for everyone

Physics Wallah Vidyapeeth Centers in India

Physics Wallah, one of India's most affordable Ed tech platforms, offers offline Vidyapeeth, a complete offline education platform, and
Pathshala classes, a hybrid education platform for student’s understanding. The platform offers an exceptional learning experience to
students, guided by a team of highly qualified instructors. These classes are designed to prepare students for rigorous competitive
exams such as IIT JEE and NEET.


PW provides education centres across 150+ centres, which means 70+ cities in India, including metropolitan cities such as New Delhi,
Noida, Faridabad, Pune, Mumbai, and many more. Vidyapeeth and Pathshala create a unique learning environment where students
thrive with like-minded peers, all driven by a shared passion for advancing together.



More than 2000 students have become a part of PW Family’s Vidyapeeth and Pathshala centres and going forward. Here’s the list of
prominent features of Vidyapeeth and Paathshala mentioned below:

2 teacher classrooms for the students
Personalized attention to all the students
Doubt Solving sessions
Interactive Learning Session
Personalized Mentor for growth
Exceptional Academic Performance


Physics Wallah at Vidyapeeth and Pathshala offers a range of advantages to enhance your preparation journey:



Solid Foundation: Our courses are structured to lay strong foundations, starting from the basics. Students gain an in-depth
understanding of concepts and can instantly clarify doubts thanks to our approachable faculty.



Hybrid Learning Session: Our Paathshala centres provide unique learning options. You can choose between attending classes in
person or online, whichever suits your schedule best. This flexibility is designed to make learning easier and more enjoyable, allowing
you to study when and how you feel most energized and motivated.



Competitive Spirit: Our comprehensive test series fosters a healthy competitive spirit among students, motivating them to improve
continually.


Exceptional Faculty: Students can learn from top-notch instructors who excel at simplifying complex concepts. Our study materials
align with the latest exam patterns and include detailed subject-wise analyses through regular testing.
